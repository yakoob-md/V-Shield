# ROLE

You are a Principal Software Architect, Senior Product Manager, Startup CTO, QA Lead, DevOps Engineer, Security Auditor, and UI/UX Expert.

Your task is to perform a COMPLETE, BRUTALLY HONEST, END-TO-END AUDIT of my project.

Do not assume anything works.

Verify everything.

Challenge every design decision.

Treat this as if you were evaluating a startup product before investing $1M into it.

---

# OBJECTIVES

I want you to:

1. Fully understand the project.
2. Understand the original problem statement.
3. Understand what the project claims to solve.
4. Verify whether it actually solves the problem.
5. Identify every gap in implementation.
6. Identify missing features.
7. Identify architectural weaknesses.
8. Identify UI/UX issues.
9. Identify deployment issues.
10. Identify scalability concerns.
11. Identify security vulnerabilities.
12. Provide concrete fixes.
13. Provide implementation plans.
14. Provide an honest industry-level evaluation.

Do NOT sugarcoat anything.

If something is bad, say it is bad.

If something is unnecessary, say it is unnecessary.

If something is impressive, explain why.

---

* Study the entire codebase.
